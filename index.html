Welcome to Camping for Dummies

Welcome! My name is Austin Glenn, and I created Camping for Dummies to help first-time campers enjoy the outdoors with confidence. Starting your camping journey can feel overwhelming, especially when there are so many gear choices, safety tips, and planning decisions to make. This website breaks everything down into simple, beginner-friendly guides that are easy to understand. Whether you're planning a family trip, a weekend getaway, or your first night under the stars, you'll find practical advice that helps you prepare, stay safe, and enjoy the experience from start to finish.
